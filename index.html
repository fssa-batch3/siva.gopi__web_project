Do you know the types of cereals?

  • When we think of grains, what comes to our mind are rice, wheat bran, corn. But actually the list is huge
  • Grains are food products grown from grasses. The study revealed that grains are grown more than any other food crop globally.
  • Natural whole grains are rich in vitamins, minerals, carbohydrates, fats, oils, and protein.
  • India is the largest producer of cereals in the world.
